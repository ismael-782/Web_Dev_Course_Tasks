Angela's Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter

Instructions

  1. Combine warm milk and yeast in a bowl. Let it sit for 5- 10 minutes until the yeast is activated.
  2. Combine the sugar, egg, egg yolk, and melted butter in a separate bowl.
  3. Combine the dry ingredients (flour) in a separate bowl.
  4. Combine the wet ingredients with the dry ingredients and mix until a dough forms.
  5. Roll out the dough to a thickness of ¼ inch.
  6. Spread the cinnamon filling over the dough, leaving a 1 inch border around the edges.
